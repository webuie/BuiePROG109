Week #1

Brookies

Created By: Wendy Buie

January 11, 2018

Is it a cookie? Is it a brownie? No, it's both! This treat combines two of the best goodies there are brownies and chocolate chip cookies.

  • Chocolate Chip Batter:
  • 10 Tbsp. softened butter
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 large egg
  • l large egg yolk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cup chocolate chips
  • Brownie Batter:
  • 10 Tbsp. softened butter
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 large egg
  • l large egg yolk
  • 1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  1. For the chocolate chip batter, with a stand mixer or hand mixer, cream together on medium speed butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth; about 2 minutes. Blend in on medium speed the egg, egg yolk, and vanilla; about 1 minute
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt
  3. Add the dry ingredients to the batter and mix until smooth
  4. Add chocolate chips and mix till combined.
  5. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes
  1. For the brownie batter, with a stand mixer or hand mixer, cream together on medium speed butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth; about 2 minutes. Blend in on medium speed the egg, egg yolk, and vanilla; about 1 minute
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, salt, and cocoa
  3. Add the dry ingredients to the batter and mix until smooth
  4. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes
  1. Preheat oven to 375 F / 190 C
  2. Portion both sets of dough into teaspoon sized balls. Grab one chocolate chip ball and one brownie batter ball, press them together and gently form into a flat, but thick cookie shape.
  3. The dough will spread while baking
  4. Place on a baking or cookie sheet at least 1 inch apart
  5. Bake the cookies for approximately 10 minutes at 375 F / 190 C
  6. Remove from oven and let the cookies cool on the pan for 2 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack
